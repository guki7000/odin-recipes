Lasagna

Description

A lasagna contains layers of pastaplates, tomato sauce and bechamel sauce.

Ingredients

  • 500g pastaplates
  • 500g mashed tomatoes
  • 500ml milk
  • 50g flour
  • 1 zucchini
  • 2 onions
  • pepper and salt
  • italian herbs
  • olive oil
  • 50g butter
  • 100g cheese

Steps

  1. Cut the onions and the zucchini in small pieces.
  2. Fry them in olive oil in a pan.
  3. Fill up the pan with the tomatoes.
  4. Add pepper, salt and herbs, as you like it.
  5. Put the butter into a pot and heat it.
  6. Once it's liquid, add the flour.
  7. While stiring the butter flour mix with a whisk constantly, add the milk constanly in small chunks
  8. When its a creamy white sauce, add pepper and salt.
  9. Put the sauces and the pasta Plates in layers into a casserole dish.
  10. add the cheese on top.
  11. Bake it for 40 minutes at 180Â°C
